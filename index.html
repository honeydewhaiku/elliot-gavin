Elliot Gavin Keenan is 26 years old and lives with his cat. He has a PhD from UCLA in Human Development & Psychology. Keenan was diagnosed with autism at 6 years old and bipolar disorder at 17. His writing is a lyrical experience of neurodivergence, wherein Keenan allows readers into his world by means of the most fragile and futile devices.


Elliot Gavin Keenan is an author of creative nonfiction.
