scrawl. verb.
to write or draw in a sprawling, awkward manner.

The Scrawl Lab is devoted to curating prose & poetry that may be experimental, quirky, fervent, intellectual yet emotional, impulsive yet articulate; work that encapsulates the psyche of the writer.
