Welcome to Infodump Pod, a podcast that first and foremost features the special interests, hyperfixations, etc of neurodivergent people. We hope that everyone can learn something from this podcast, thus illustrating that special interests and hyperfixations are not merely pathological phenomena but are also, in their own way, adaptive and useful. Far from being merely symptoms to tick off the boxes in making a diagnosis of Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), ADHD, or another condition, these interests constitute our truest passions - and they're not just trains or timetables, either! Neurodivergent people can provide a wealth of knowledge in an astoundingly wide variety of areas, and this podcast provides an opportunity for us to do just that.

Infodump Pod was created by Elliot Gavin Keenan, author of the memoir On Being Insane and PhD student at the University of California, Los Angeles.

If you want to be a guest on this podcast, please contact me via email at egkeenan@g.ucla.edu.
