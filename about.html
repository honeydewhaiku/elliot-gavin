The seemingly premeditated rhythm of his pacing, back and forth, evokes the mechanical motions of a Newton's cradle. Click... click. Yet there is a degree of self-awareness in his graceless stride - perhaps a consequence of being conscious of the awkwardness of his own movements from an early age. He is compact in stature. The impression he gives is androgynous if not for the dark stubble that traces his jaw. His hair - thick, coarse curls the color of charcoal - grazes the top of his perfectly round spectacles. He wears idiosyncrasy like a favorite t-shirt.
